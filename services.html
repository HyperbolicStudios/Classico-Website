Services

While every project is different, the steps below give a sense of what to expect when working with me. Depending on your needs, I can provide you with 2D design concept plans, 3D visualization drawings and planting plans. I am happy to personally source quality plant material from selected nurseries and oversee the planting. In addition to recommending contractors for hardscape installations, I offer a project management service.


1. Design Services

​


Step 1. Initial design consultation

I listen to your requirements and ideas. As it can be challenging for some clients to visualize what they would like, I try to interpret your preferences and style, and help brainstorm options that might suit you. ​

Step 2. Site analysis

After we sign the design contract, I conduct a site analysis. At this time, I take measurements and photographs. Complex sites with significant level changes might require a professional survey.

​ Step 3. Design concept plans

Based on the site analysis and keeping in mind your goals and budget, I prepare:

  • A CAD 2D concept plan drawn to scale that illustrates the new space layout. This includes hardscape areas (patios, decks, paths), planting areas, major trees, lawns, and any structures to be included (fences, walls, water features, pergolas, trellises, large planters, vegetable beds). This plans does not include specific plant choices; however, a general planting theme is indicated.

I also provide 3D renderings to help you visualize the designed area, plantings, hardscapes, as well as levels of sun and shade during the day.

Inspirational pictures and/or drawings of structures such as fences, pergolas, walls, fireplaces, fire pits, privacy screens.

Step 4. Detailed planting plan

Upon approval of the concept plan, I prepare: A detailed planting plan with a list of all plants to be sourced, including quantities and planting instructions.

2. Project Oversight Services

This is the best way to ensure that your project is built according to your vision and meets your high quality standards.

I help you obtain competitive bids and select a trusted contractor.

I assist in materials procurement (i.e. selecting paving or wood for fencing and other structures).

Although the contractor administers the contract, I do site visits to ensure that the project is steered according to what is specified in the design, and am available to answer questions that might arise during the installation process.
